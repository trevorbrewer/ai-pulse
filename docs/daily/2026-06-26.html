Safety Research and Adversarial Testing

What happened after 2,000 people tried to hack my AI assistantSimon Willison

Simon Willison summarized a public adversarial challenge run by Fernando Irarrázaval on hackmyclaw.com, in which participants attempted to extract secrets from an AI assistant called OpenClaw by sending it emails.
